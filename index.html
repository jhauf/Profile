Jane Hauf

I am a full-stack software engineer who enjoys creating products that are both functional and beautiful.

I have always enjoyed building things, a passion that started from a young age. When I was 10 years old I taught myself HTML to build pages for my Neopets. I studied Business at UC Berkeley's Haas School to hone my creative problem solving and analytical skills.

I enjoyed and excelled at the technical aspects of my job and decided to switch to the software engineering track. I taught myself programming and I attended App Academy's selective full-stack engineering program to formalize my knowledge. I am passionate about software engineering and excited to work on meaningful projects.
